# Virus Boss Mechanic Notes

## Best Boss Picks

### Pox-Brick Boss

Inspired by poxvirus/mpox imagery: large mature particles and smaller immature crescent/sphere forms.

- Visual read: big rounded brick with plated armor and glowing seams.
- Core pattern: slow, tanky, screen-control boss.
- Phase idea: armor plates break off and become smaller crescent adds.
- Attack ideas: shield wall, pustule mines, heavy body slam, short-range burst cloud.
- Difficulty hook: player must strip armor before the weak core is vulnerable.

### Filovirus Ribbon Boss

Inspired by Ebola/Marburg-like filament particles.

- Visual read: long flexible strand, curled loop, or segmented neon ribbon.
- Core pattern: movement boss with sweeping arcs.
- Phase idea: one long body becomes several shorter strands.
- Attack ideas: whip sweep, spiral coil trap, lane-filling wall, split-and-rejoin dash.
- Difficulty hook: only the head and tail are safe weak points while the body remains hazardous.

### Phage Siege Boss

Inspired by bacteriophage forms, used as visual/mechanical inspiration rather than a literal human virus.

- Visual read: geometric head, stalk/tail injector, spider-like landing legs.
- Core pattern: positional boss that pins and controls space.
- Phase idea: anchors to vessel walls, then relocates after taking enough damage.
- Attack ideas: injector beam, tethered DNA spear, landing-leg stomp, summoned drone phages.
- Difficulty hook: destroy legs or tail segments to interrupt large attacks.

## Strong Mini-Boss Picks

### Adenovirus Prism

Inspired by adenovirus clusters and faceted capsids.

- Visual read: purple crystalline orb with rotating facets or surface fibers.
- Core pattern: rotating shield and weak-point timing.
- Attack ideas: radial spike burst, bouncing facet shards, rotating invulnerability panels.
- Difficulty hook: only matching-color shots or attacks damage the exposed face.

### Rabies Bullet

Inspired by rabies/rhabdovirus bullet-shaped particles.

- Visual read: fast capsule or bullet with a flared base.
- Core pattern: charge-and-ricochet mini-boss.
- Attack ideas: telegraphed lane charge, wall bounce, shockwave after impact.
- Difficulty hook: creates panic pressure by forcing quick dodges through blood-cell traffic.

### HIV Budding Factory

Inspired by HIV particles budding from immune cells.

- Visual read: infected cell mound that grows virus buds.
- Core pattern: support boss or arena hazard.
- Attack ideas: buds mature into adds, infected patches regenerate shields, pulse disables a player buff briefly.
- Difficulty hook: the player must choose between hitting the boss or pruning new buds before they detach.

## My Recommendation

Use the Pox-Brick Boss as the first true boss because it is readable, chunky, and naturally supports phases. Use Adenovirus Prism as a mini-boss before it, then save Filovirus Ribbon or Phage Siege for a later, harder encounter with more movement pressure.
